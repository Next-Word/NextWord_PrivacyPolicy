Nguyen Vo Dang Cao built the NextWord game as a Free game. This game is provided by Nguyen Vo Dang Cao at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Game Introduction: Challenging your vocabulary together with friends and relaxing your brain. "Nextword" is a word game which you can both check your words and enjoy game play with your friends. You and your friends will have a chill time for either reminding your vocabulary or challenging each other. 

Features: 
- Easy to play with simple functions.
- You can easily create room for playing and sharing room ID to your friends. 
- You can also make the game more challenging by setting random turn.

How to play:
- Create room or join room in the Play Game box;
- Share room ID to your friends or join room by room ID from your friends;
- Set time span and player limitation before starting game;
- Each player will have time to enter one word and submit before other takes turn.
